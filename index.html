  1. Folate :  Vitamin B9, also known as folate, is essential for the proper functioning of cells and the growth of tissues. It helps in the production of fatty acids, which are important compounds for preserving the skin’s natural protective layer. Its potential in improving skin health is gaining recognition due to its role in balancing hormones and serving as an effective antioxidant. Scientific studies have demonstrated that DIM can aid in reducing inflammation and supporting clearer skin. A significant advantage of DIM is its capability to alleviate hormonal imbalances that are often associated with skin conditions such as acne and excessive oil production. By fostering a more uniform hormonal environment, DIM helps in achieving a balanced and glowing complexion. Vitamin B12 :  Vitamin B12 plays a crucial role in preserving skin health by promoting the generation of new cells and potentially diminishing the visibility of imperfections and an uneven skin tone. Research indicates that it can boost skin moisture levels, enhance flexibility, and stimulate collagen synthesis, essential for youthful skin. Its presence in the formula strengthens the product's ability to pamper and revitalize the skin, resulting in a radiant complexion.
